Abstract

The Toyota Boshoku cyberattack represents a critical Business Email Compromise (BEC) case, where attackers exploited human trust and communication systems.

This report analyzes vulnerabilities, impacts, and preventive strategies in modern organizations.

↑ ↓

Executive Summary

Incident

Fake executive email triggered fund transfer.

Loss

Millions lost due to weak verification.

Cause

Human error and poor controls.

Background Information

Toyota Boshoku operates in a high-volume financial environment where frequent transactions create opportunities for attackers to blend malicious actions.

Industry

Automotive manufacturing

Risk

High transaction volume → easier fraud
